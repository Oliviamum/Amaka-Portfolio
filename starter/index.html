About Me
Amaka Metu joined the Department of Business and Management at the University of Sheffield International College (USIC) in Spring 2017. She is the Curriculum Leader for Business and Management. She served as Interim Head of Business in 2019 and as co-ordinator of Pre Masters Teaching (responsible for all Pre-Master-Programmes) at USIC. She has degrees in Management Studies, and Economics.
